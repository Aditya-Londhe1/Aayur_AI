## ğŸ“Š Understanding Your Results

### Dosha Analysis

**What is Dosha?**
- Ayurvedic body constitution
- Three types: Vata, Pitta, Kapha
- Everyone has all three
- One or two usually dominant

**Vata** (Air + Space)
- **Characteristics**: Creative, energetic, quick-thinking
- **When Balanced**: Enthusiastic, flexible, imaginative
- **When Imbalanced**: Anxious, restless, digestive issues
- **Symptoms**: Dry skin, constipation, insomnia, anxiety

**Pitta** (Fire + Water)
- **Characteristics**: Intelligent, focused, determined
- **When Balanced**: Sharp mind, good digestion, warm body
- **When Imbalanced**: Irritable, inflammatory conditions
- **Symptoms**: Acidity, skin rashes, anger, inflammation

**Kapha** (Earth + Water)
- **Characteristics**: Calm, stable, nurturing
- **When Balanced**: Strong, steady, compassionate
- **When Imbalanced**: Lethargic, weight gain, congestion
- **Symptoms**: Sluggishness, weight gain, mucus, depression
